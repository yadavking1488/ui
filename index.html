About WildLife

Did you know that the deepest known part of the ocean is the Challenger Deep in the Mariana Trench, reaching a depth of about 36,070 feet (10,994 meters)? That's like stacking more than six Empire State Buildings on top of each other underwater! The fascinating life forms down there have adapted to extreme pressure and darkness in ways that still leave scientists amazed.
